Our Services

Sub Soil Investigation

Detailed subsoil investigation for all types of civil engineering projects, including land and marine environments. We analyze soil conditions, stratigraphy, and groundwater levels to support foundation and earthworks design.

Rock Anchoring (Passive & Prestressed)

Installation of rock anchors using passive and prestressed methods to stabilize slopes, retaining walls, and heavy structural foundations. Designed to enhance stability and resist earth pressures in challenging terrains.

Micro-Piling

Precision micro-piling solutions to reinforce foundations in restricted or sensitive areas. Ideal for underpinning existing structures, improving load-bearing capacity, and minimizing site disturbance.

Drilling & Pressure Grouting

Advanced drilling techniques combined with pressure grouting for dams, water-retaining structures, and heavy machinery foundations. Improves ground strength, reduces permeability, and fills voids to enhance structural safety.

Drilling in Concrete & Rock

All types of drilling services for concrete and rock, including borehole drilling for heavy machinery foundations and bolt installations. Ensures precise hole creation with minimal structural impact.

Percussion Drilling

Efficient percussion drilling for rock and concrete to create boreholes for anchors, piles, and other geotechnical applications. Reliable and fast, suitable for various civil engineering projects.

About Us

With a commitment to precision and safety, we offer reliable, site-specific engineering expertise across Maharashtra.
