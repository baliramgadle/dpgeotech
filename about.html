About DP Construction & Geotechnical Engineering

Your trusted partner in building foundations that last.

Who We Are

DP Construction & Geotechnical Engineering is a leading civil engineering firm based in Maharashtra, specializing in comprehensive soil and marine investigations, advanced rock anchoring (both passive and prestressed), micro-piling, drilling, and pressure grouting services. Our expert team of engineers and technicians combines in-depth technical knowledge with practical field experience to provide reliable, precise, and innovative geotechnical solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower the construction industry with scientifically backed insights and cutting-edge technologies. We deliver accurate soil and subsoil investigations, tailored foundation and anchoring solutions, and expert consultancy to ensure safe, sustainable, and cost-effective infrastructure development. We are dedicated to building trust through quality, precision, and timely project execution.
