About Us

We specialize in Subsoil Investigation, Drilling, and Geotechnical Solutions for all types of civil engineering projects. Our services include pressure grouting for dams and water-retaining structures, foundation drilling for heavy machinery, micro-piling, rock anchoring, and percussion drilling. With a commitment to precision and safety, we offer reliable, site-specific engineering expertise across Maharashtra.
